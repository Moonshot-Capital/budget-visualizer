How it works

1. Setup Your Profile

Start by entering your job title, selecting your preferred currency, and adding your monthly or annual net income. The visualizer will automatically calculate the matching values.

2. Add Expenses

Use the "New Expense" form to add custom expenses, or click on the "Common Expenses" chips for quick additions. You can double-click any expense in the list to edit it, or drag and drop to reorder them.

3. Visualize & Export

The bar at the top updates in real-time, showing how much of your income goes where. Once you are happy, use the Export buttons at the bottom to download a PDF report or a portrait image tailored for Instagram.
